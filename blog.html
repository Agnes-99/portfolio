My Journey in Tech

Here are a few highlights from my journey in the tech world, showcasing some key events, projects, and moments of growth. Each experience has shaped my perspective on how technology is transforming industries and our daily lives.

At a recent Google event, I delved deep into cutting-edge technologies such as AI-driven industrial optimization, JAX for high-performance machine learning, and Google Cloud's latest innovations. I also explored the emerging role of AI agents and how they're revolutionizing automation across industries. It was a powerful learning experience that reinforced the importance of AI in shaping the future.

This digital art piece represents a collaborative project I worked on with an innovative digital art company. The focus was on creating a seamless user experience for students, combining functionality with an aesthetically pleasing design. It was a rewarding project that sharpened my skills in user-centric design.

I had the chance to dive into Amazon's cloud technologies and explore their cutting-edge work in robotics. Gaining hands-on experience with these tools provided me with a deeper understanding of how cloud computing is intertwined with robotics, and the potential this combination holds for automating industries in the future.

One of the books that has been shaping my understanding of AI and its historical context is *Nexus: From the Stone Age to AI*. This thought-provoking read explores the evolution of intelligence, both human and artificial, and offers a compelling perspective on the future of AI. It's been an essential resource in deepening my insights into the broader journey of human and technological evolution.
